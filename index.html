Demon Slayer

Protagonist

Tanjiro Kamado

The main protagonist of the series. A kind-hearted boy who becomes a Demon Slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister, Nezuko.

Protagonist

Nezuko Kamado

Tanjiro's younger sister who was turned into a demon. She retains her human emotions and fights alongside her brother.

Protagonist

Zenitsu Agatsuma

A fellow Demon Slayer who is cowardly but immensely powerful when asleep. He wields the Thunder Breathing techniques.

Protagonist

Inosuke Hashibira

A Demon Slayer known for his boar-like behavior and beast-like fighting style. He uses the Beast Breathing techniques.

Strongest Demon Slayer

Yorichi Tsugikuni

The most powerful Demon Slayer in history, known for his Sun Breathing techniques. He played a crucial role in defeating Muzan.

Flower Hashira

Kanao Kocho

The Flower Hashira who is known for her quiet demeanor and mastery of Flower Breathing techniques.

Mist Hashira

Muichiro Tokito

The Mist Hashira, known for his calm and detached demeanor, Muichiro despite his forgetful nature, is a formidable swordsman with swift and unpredictable mist-based techniques.

Water Hashira

Giyu Tomioka

The Water Hashira who is known for his stoic demeanor and mastery of Water Breathing techniques.

Flame Hashira

Kyojuro Rengoku

The Flame Hashira who is known for his fiery passion and powerful Flame Breathing techniques.

Wind Hashira

Sanemi Shinazugawa

The Wind Hashira who is known for his abrasive personality and mastery of Wind Breathing techniques.

Love Hashira

Mitsuri Kanroji

The Love Hashira who is known for her cheerful demeanor and mastery of Love Breathing techniques.

Insect Hashira

Shinobu Kocho

The Insect Hashira who is known for her calm demeanor and mastery of Insect Breathing techniques.

Stone Hashira

Gyomei Himejima

The Stone Hashira known for their immense strength and mastery of Stone Breathing techniques. They are a formidable figure in the fight against demons.

Serpent Hashira

Obanai Iguro

The Serpent Hashira, known for his unique fighting style and mastery of Serpent Breathing techniques. He exhibits agility and precision in battles against demons.

Sound Hashira

Tengen Uzui

The Sound Hashira, known for his flamboyant personality and mastery of Sound Breathing techniques. Tengen Uzui is a powerful and flashy warrior who fights with explosive precision.

Antagonist

Muzan Kibutsuji

The primary antagonist of the series. A powerful demon responsible for the death of Tanjiro's family and the cause of Nezuko's demonization.
